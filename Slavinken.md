Recipe for slavinken
====================
A Dutch classic.

Ingredients
-----------
- 1 box of slavinken

Algorithm
---------
1. Bake the slavinken
	- Pan: Big 7-ply DeMeyere
	- Duration: 21 minutes
	- Power: 6
	- Oil: Medium
2. Turn every 2 or 3 minutes. Use a spatula, not pinchers.

Criticism
---------
- Hard to prevent the sleeve from shredding. Maybe pre-heat the pan?
