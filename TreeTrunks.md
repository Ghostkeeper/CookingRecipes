Recipe voor tree trunks
-----------------------

Requires box of tree trunks (meat loaf rolls) pre-spiced from Albert Heijn.

- Pan: Big 7-ply DeMeyere
- Duration: 18 minutes
- Power: 6
- Oil: Generous

1. Turn every 5 minutes.

Criticism
---------
- Turn more often.
- Slightly burnt on 2 sides, so shorten duration next time.
