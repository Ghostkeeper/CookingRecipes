Recipe for cordon bleu
======================
Easy and delicious, but it takes a significant time to cook and properly melt the cheese.

Ingredients
-----------
- One tray of cordon bleu filets (350g)

Algorithm
---------
1. Air-fry:
- 12 minutes
- 180 degrees

Criticism
---------
- Too long. Maybe 8 or 10 minutes?
