Recipe for bratwurst
====================
These are pre-packaged saucages that just need to be baked.

Ingredients
-----------
- 4 sausages

Algorithm
---------
1. Bake the sausages.
	- Pan: Big 7-ply DeMeyere
	- Duration: 12 minutes
	- Power: 7
	- Oil: Medium
2. Turn every 3 minutes.

Criticism
---------
- Probably healthier to reduce oil. These leak a lot of fat themselves.
