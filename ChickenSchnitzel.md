Recipe for chicken schnitzel
============================
Easy and extremely delicious, but high calorie.

Ingredients
-----------
- One tray of pre-made chicken schnitzel (250g)

Algorithm
---------
1. Bake:
	- Pan: Big 7-ply DeMeyere
	- Duration: 11 minutes
	- Power: 6
	- Oil: Medium

Criticism
---------
- Lot of variation in burntness depending on oil? Needs tweaking.
