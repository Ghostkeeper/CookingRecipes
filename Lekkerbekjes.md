Recipe for lekkerbekjes
=======================
How long to bake frozen lekkerbekjes.

Ingredients
-----------
- 1 box of lekkerbekjes (~240g)

Algorithm
---------
1. Bake the lekkerbekjes
	- Pan: Big 7-ply DeMeyere
	- Duration: 7 minutes
	- Power: 7
	- Oil: Medium
2. Turn 3 times.

Criticism
---------
* Needs to bake way longer at lower fire.
